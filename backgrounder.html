Wyncode Backgrounder

ABOUT US

Wyncode is the first coding boot camp accredited by the Florida Department of Education’s Commission for Independent Education. Wyncode is a nine-week long coding boot camp that teaches students the five most used coding languages: Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, CSS and HTML. The course is open to all students, from beginners to advanced. The course begins with the basics, and at the end of the nine-week course, students present a finished web application that showcases what they learned.

MISSION STATEMENT

We believe in promoting programming literacy using a disruptive education model and feel that a boot camp environment is the best way to learn practical and relevant coding skills quickly.

Wyncode Academy separates itself from other coding boot camps by focusing on teaching coding skills and the business and leadership skills you need to succeed, whether you plan to start your own company or work for an established one. We are also the only boot camp located in the dynamic, up and coming tech hub of Miami, which is uniquely positioned to become a major tech center due to its entrepreneurial climate and close links to Latin America.

ABOUT THE PROGRAM

The day begins with a concept explained by an instructor and is followed by a practical example of how it is used. Then students are given time to apply this concept and practice. The end of the day is devoted to business development and mentoring sessions.

A typical day:

  • 9:30 a.m. – Stand ups: Wyncoders discuss their plan for the day and how to overcome any obstacles.
  • 10 a.m. to 12 p.m. – Tech lecture
  • 12 p.m. to 1 p.m. – Lunch break
  • 1 p.m. to 6 p.m. – Project-based learning/coding time. Instructors and teaching assistants are available to help Wyncoders with any issues.
  • 3:30 p.m. to 4 p.m. – Two times a week, a business speaker joins Wyncode Academy for an intimate chat. These speakers include CEOs, Chief Technical Officers, entrepreneurs, recruiters and tech industry leaders.
  • 6 p.m. – Wyncoders are welcome to stay in the Wyncode Academy space as late as they want to work on projects.
  • Wyncode Academy encourages a balanced lifestyle and optional evening events include a run crew on Mondays, Yoga on Wednesdays and more.
  • Wyncoders are notified about any tech events that are held in Miami.

PRICING AND FINANCING

Wyncode’s tuition is $10,000. This price includes two weeks at the end of the program for students to continue working in the Wyncode Academy space, allowing them access to the mentors and peers.

Wyncode requires a $1,000 deposit when accepted to the program. This is non-refundable. The full balance is due before the first day of class.

Wyncode and Climb have partnered to provide students with loans in order to finance their studies.

EVENTS

Pitch Day

Twice a year, at the end of each coding cohort, there is a pitch day. This is the final showcase where Wyncoders launch their web applications, demo and pitch their idea to local developers and entrepreneurs. The event is free to the public.
